Empower your decision-making with comprehensive ecommerce reporting.

The primary objective of ecommerce reporting is to compile data that highlights your company’s performance. However, this effort becomes futile if excessive time is consumed in report construction, leaving insufficient time for leveraging the insights to drive marketing strategies. Here’s where Netsetgo, our ecommerce reporting tool, proves invaluable.

Consolidate your data into a unified report

Netsetgo enables you to aggregate sales data from all your channels into a comprehensive sales report, providing you with a quick overview of your ecommerce strategy's performance. The best part? It's fully automated! Say goodbye to hours spent on manual data entry - we handle that for you!

Enhance the visual appeal of your data

Let's face it, nobody enjoys sifting through a dull, impersonal report that requires a magnifying glass to decipher. That's precisely why our ecommerce reporting tool isn't just a time-saving marvel; it also generates reports that are both easy to comprehend and visually stunning!

Tailor your ecommerce report to your preferences

We recognize that every client has unique requirements for ecommerce reporting, and at Netsetgo, we fully understand this diversity. Whether it's incorporating your logo, crafting widgets from scratch, or integrating images, comments, and insights, virtually every aspect of your ecommerce report can be customized to suit your specific needs.

A comprehensive data hub encompassing all aspects

Supporting over 30 integrations, Netsetgo enables seamless connection with your preferred platforms directly into our ecommerce reporting tool. Can’t find the specific integration you need? No problem. With our CSV integration, simply plug your file into your Netsetgo account, and voila! All your data is readily accessible in one convenient location!
